Welcome

Welcome to my practice


I am an HCPC registered clinical & counselling psychologist based in south west London offering face-to-face and online sessions in English, French and Spanish. 

I am a caring, compassionate and experienced Psychologist with over 14 years extensive experience working in the NHS, and the voluntary and private sectors with children, families and adults with a broad range of emotional, social, behavioural and mental health difficulties.

I offer an empathic, warm and supportive space, where it feels safe to explore, express and process thoughts and feelings, without fear of judgement.

I am passionate about supporting and empowering the children, young people and adults I work with to make and sustain meaningful changes in their lives. I believe the therapeutic space should be a safe and nurturing place which enables to process difficult feelings and to move forward with realistic and fulfilling ways to manage challenges. 

As an integrative practitioner I am able to draw on several therapeutic schools of thought and from a range of psychological approaches so that I can support clients in a way that is most useful to them. I work creatively and with an explorative style including narrative therapy, integrative psychotherapy, systemic and cognitive behavioural therapy.

I work part-time for the NHS in a specialist mental health setting for families, children and adolescents alongside my private practice.

I am fluent and able to conduct therapy in English, French and Spanish.

Therapy

I am experienced in working with all areas of mental health distress and life challenges with adults, children and adolescents. Learn more.

I offer 50-minute sessions in my practice in south west London or online via Skype, Zoom or Facetime.

EMDR

I offer EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) Therapy to adults, young people and children. EMDR is a specific type of therapy designed to alleviate the distress associated with traumatic memories and to recover from symptoms and emotional suffering that are the result of disturbing life experiences. Learn more.

Psychodiagnostic assessment

I offer specialist psychological assessment and cognitive testing for children, young people and adults. Learn more.
