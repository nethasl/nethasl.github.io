EMDR

I offer EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) Therapy to adults, young people and children. EMDR is a specific type of therapy designed to alleviate the distress associated with traumatic memories and to recover from symptoms and emotional suffering that are the result of disturbing life experiences.

EMDR requires a minimum commitment of 6 sessions.

I am skilled at doing therapeutic interventions for Post-traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) following: 

Accidents
Assaults and victims of crime
Witnessing life-threatening experiences
Vicarious traumatisation
Victims of mass casualty incidents such as terrorism
