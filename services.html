Therapy for adults, children and adolescents

I am experienced in working with all areas of mental health distress and life challenges, I can help with:

Low mood, loss, bereavement and depression;
Anxiety, stress and worry (incl. Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder & trauma, Panic, Generalised Anxiety, OCD, Social Anxiety, Specific Phobias)
Interpersonal, personality and relationship difficulties
Behavioural problems (incl. anger management, addiction, food issues, self-harm, unhelpful repetitive patterns);
Life stage challenges (incl. professional issues, career/work/school issues, parenthood, divorce/separation); 
Adjustment to the impact of life transitions: job and house moves, psychology issues related to illness and health needs, adjustment to a family member's additional needs, antenatal and postnatal therapy.
Physical Health, pain and medically unexplained physical conditions
Supporting self-esteem and resilience

EMDR

I offer EMDR therapy to adults, young people and children. EMDR is a specific type of therapy for helping with psychological trauma and involves a particular treatment protocol and techniques.
I am skilled at doing therapeutic interventions for Post-traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) following: 

Accidents
Assaults and victims of crime
Witnessing life-threatening experiences
Vicarious traumatisation
Victims of mass casualty incidents such as terrorism

Fees

Each 50-minute therapy session of face-to-face of video-session is £100.

EMDR sessions are £110 and vary between 50-75 minutes. 

Please enquire about Diagnostic Assessments (fees will vary depending of the needs and length of the assessment required).

I am registered and recognised by the following healthcare insurance providers: AXA PPP, AVIVA, ALLIANZ, VITALITY HEALTH, WPA.   

International bank transfers are possible (for payments in Euros and Dollars) available on agreement. 

If you are on a low-income please mention this when getting in touch, as I have a limited number of concession spaces. 

In case of cancellation, I would ask you to notify me 48 hours in advance, otherwise the session will be due in full.
